About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Centre for Healthy Brain Ageing (CHeBA), UNSW, where my research focuses on MRI data analysis and the effects of aging, neurodegeneration, and dementia. I received my PhD from UNSW Sydney in 2024, specializing in computer vision and machine learning. My doctoral work involved developing AI models for Whole Slide Image analysis, visual anomaly detection, and agricultural image analysis.
