About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Centre for Healthy Brain Ageing (CHeBA), UNSW. I received my PhD from UNSW Sydney in 2024, specializing in computer vision and machine learning.

My Research Interests:

  • Whole Slide Image Analysis
  • Visual Anomaly Detection
  • Grain Image Analysis
  • MRI Data Analysis
